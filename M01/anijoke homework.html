DISH NAMECONTENT DESCRIPTION
Palusami Taro leaves Palusami is dish that consist in cooking of the taro leaves and wrapping of Tin Meat inside.
Ba ba Taro stem The Ba ba is also a fijian traditional dish where the stem of the taro leave gets cooked till it gets the right temperature and then we pour the tin fish in it and then about 15 minutes it will be ready to be eaten.
