description of a cat

Cats have a variety of fur patterns, including striped, tortoiseshell, and calico. They have long tails for balance, and their bodies are flexible. Cats have large eyes that help them see well at night, and their triangular ears can turn quickly to locate sounds. They also have long whiskers on either side of their face that give them a sensitive sense of touch.
